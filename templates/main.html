Coastal Flyrodders

A Northern New Jersey Saltwater Flyfishing Club whose primary goal is to have fun and learn as much as possible from one another about great east coast saltwater fly fishing and anywhere else that affords fly fishing for big fish.

Meetings

Meetings are held 7:30PM the third Tuesday of every month at the Wyckoff Larkin House 380 Godwin Ave, Wyckoff, NJ 07481 If you have questions about any meeting please post it on the message board and we will answer you.
